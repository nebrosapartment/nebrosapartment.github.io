The Area

Western Crete at your doorstep

Voukolies is perfectly placed for the finest beaches, towns, and landscapes.

Beach

Falasarna

One of Greece's finest beaches. Turquoise water, golden sand, unforgettable sunsets.

~25 min drive

City

Chania Old Town

Venetian harbour, vibrant market, excellent restaurants and bars.

~30 min drive

Nature

White Mountains

Dramatic peaks, gorges, and traditional villages. Samaria Gorge nearby.

~30 min drive

Beach

Balos Lagoon

Iconic turquoise lagoon at the tip of the Gramvousa peninsula.

~40 min drive

Village

Kolymvari

Charming fishing village with tavernas and a lovely seafront.

~15 min drive

Local

Voukolies Village

Traditional village with taverna, café, and a famous weekly market.

Walking distance

Beach

Elafonisi

World-famous pink-sand beach and turquoise lagoon. One of Europe’s most breathtaking shores.

~45 min drive

Town

Paleochora

Charming seaside town on the south-west coast, with sandy beaches, tavernas, and a relaxed bohemian vibe.

~50 min drive

Town

Kissamos

Lively market town with a good beach, excellent tavernas, and the main port for boat trips to Balos and Gramvousa.

~20 min drive

Sport & Outdoor

Stay active

World-class outdoor experiences for all levels.

🏊
Swimming

Pristine beaches 20–35 min away. Falasarna & Balos among Greece's best.

🥾
Hiking

Samaria Gorge, Imbros Gorge, and dozens of mountain trails nearby.

🚴
Cycling

Scenic routes through olive groves and coastal roads. Rentals in Chania.

🤿
Snorkelling

Crystal-clear waters with rich marine life along the Kissamos coast.

🏄
Watersports

Windsurfing, kayaking, and paddleboarding at nearby beach centres.

🧗
Rock Climbing

The White Mountains offer excellent routes for all abilities.

🎣
Fishing

Join local fishermen or rent a small boat from Kolymvari harbour.

🐎
Horse Riding

Guided rides through olive groves and coastal landscapes near Chania.

Cultural

Rich history & heritage

Crete is one of the Mediterranean's greatest cultural destinations.

🏺
Minoan Civilisation

Palace of Knossos near Heraklion — Europe's oldest civilisation. A half-day trip worth every minute.

⛵
Venetian Chania

Old harbour, lighthouse, and covered market — living remnants of Venice's rule. Stunning at sunset.

⛪
Byzantine Churches

Hundreds of small churches and monasteries dot the countryside, many with original frescoes.

🫒
Olive Oil Tradition

Visit a local press, taste estate oils, and discover a tradition going back 4,000 years.

🎶
Cretan Music & Dance

The lyra, mandinades poetry, and pentozalis dance — catch a live performance in any village.

🛒
Voukolies Market

Every Saturday, one of Crete's largest traditional markets — fresh produce, honey, herbs.

🍷
Cretan Cuisine

The Mediterranean diet at its finest. Dakos, fresh fish, local cheeses, exceptional wines.

🏛
Archaeological Museum

Chania's museum houses extraordinary finds from Minoan to Byzantine eras.
